Brazil Overview

Brazil is the largest country in South America, known for its vast Amazon Rainforest, diverse culture, and vibrant cities like SÃ£o Paulo and Rio de Janeiro. As the world's fifth-largest country by both area and population, Brazil has a rich blend of indigenous, African, and European influences. Portuguese is the official language, making Brazil unique in Latin America. Its economy is the largest in the region, driven by agriculture, mining, and industry. Famous for its love of football and lively festivals like Carnival, Brazil is a global cultural and environmental powerhouse, though it faces challenges like deforestation and inequality.
